Climate Change

Facts

-Earth's average surface temperature has risen 2 degrees Fahrenheit since the late 19th century

-Ocean acidification is a result of excess carbon dioxide being absorbed into the water and harming the marine life

-The warming of Earth has caused glaciers to melt at an alarming rate of 272 billion tons annually since 2000 (average)

Causes

Fossil fuels are a main reason for carbon dioxide release into the atmosphere. Deforestation also plays a large part in the rising temperatures as it prevents the Earth from absorbing carbon dioxide. Everyday things like cars, planes, and heating all can use fossil fuels.

Solutions

Renewable energy types like wind and solar are great solutions because they are inexhaustible. Insulating is another way to prevent leaving large carbon dioxide footprints as it mitigates the use of heating. Investing in energy effecient appliances can also reduce the emission of carbon dioxide.
